The Holy Grail is a Christian relic that has been the subject of many myths and legends for centuries. The Grail is traditionally thought to be the cup that Jesus drank from at the Last Supper and the one used by Joseph of Arimathea to catch his blood during the crucifixion.

Heading

The Grail is also said to have miraculous healing powers, and in most texts, it is considered to be an artifact that can grant its bearer eternal life, abundance, healing, desires, and unlimited youth.
